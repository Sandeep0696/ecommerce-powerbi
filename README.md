</ul>

<h2>📌 Key KPIs</h2>
<ul>
<li><b>Total Revenue:</b> $15.84M</li>
<li><b>Average Rating:</b> 4.07</li>
<li><b>Delayed Orders:</b> 7,827</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Key Insights</h2>

<h3>🔹 Revenue Trends</h3>
<ul>
<li>Strong year-on-year growth from 2016 to 2018</li>
<li>Peak revenue observed in 2018 (~$8.6M)</li>
</ul>

<h3>🔹 Seasonal Patterns</h3>
<ul>
<li>Highest sales in Q2 (~$4.8M)</li>
<li>Decline observed in Q4 indicating seasonal variation</li>
</ul>

<h3>🔹 Delivery Performance</h3>
<ul>
<li>Delays increase during peak sales periods</li>
<li>Bulky categories (e.g., furniture) show higher delay rates</li>
</ul>

<h3>🔹 Payment Analysis</h3>
<ul>
<li>Credit cards dominate transactions</li>
<li>Growing adoption of alternative payment methods</li>
</ul>

<h3>🔹 Product Ratings</h3>
<ul>
<li>Top-performing categories include CDs, fashion, and books</li>
<li>Low-rated categories highlight improvement opportunities</li>
</ul>

<h3>🔹 Geographic Insights</h3>
<ul>
<li>São Paulo (SP) contributes the highest revenue (~$5.9M)</li>
<li>Emerging states show growth potential</li>
</ul>
